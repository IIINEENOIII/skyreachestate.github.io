The SkyReach process

  1. Short call (10–15 min): understand your goal and constraints.
  2. Property review: quick walk-through or remote review depending on situation.
  3. Strategy + numbers: cash offer and/or alternative structures if they fit.
  4. Closing plan: title work + payoff coordination + chosen timeline.
  5. Close and move on: you pick the date; we handle the details.
If foreclosure is imminent, speed matters. We can move fast, but we’ll always be honest about what’s realistic.

FAQ

Quick answers to common seller questions.

How do you determine your offer?

We look at location, condition, repairs, market demand, holding costs, and comparable sales—then we explain the math.

Do I need to clean or repair anything?

No. We buy as-is. Take what you want and leave the rest (we’ll confirm details upfront).

How fast can you close?

Depending on title and payoff timing, closings can happen in as little as 7–14 days. Some situations require more time.

Can you help if I’m in foreclosure or bankruptcy?

We can evaluate options quickly. In active bankruptcy or legal deadlines, we recommend involving qualified counsel.

Will you list my home or do showings?

No showings are required for an as-is investor sale. If a different strategy is better, we’ll tell you.

Are creative finance options “safe”?

They can help in specific cases, but they involve risk and must be documented correctly. We’ll explain pros/cons before anything moves forward.

Timeline-based selling.

Different timelines change what’s possible. Here’s what “fast” vs “flexible” usually looks like.

0–7 days (Emergency / deadline)

  • Best for foreclosure deadlines or urgent relocation.
  • We prioritize payoff info, title, and immediate scheduling.
  • Requires seller responsiveness + clear access.
Ideal when speed is more important than maximizing price.

7–14 days (Fast close)

  • Common for as-is investor closings.
  • Enough time for title + payoff coordination.
  • Still fast; minimal disruption.
Great balance of speed and certainty.

14–30 days (Flexible close)

  • Room for planning move-out and cleaning.
  • Helps if there are small title items to resolve.
  • More options become viable.
Good for most non-emergency situations.

30–60+ days (Maximize options)

  • Potentially supports broader strategies (depending on market).
  • More time to coordinate estate/probate timelines.
  • More time for “best net” path selection.
Best when you’re not time-pressured.

What slows a closing

  • Unknown liens / unpaid taxes
  • Missing heirs / probate delays
  • Payoff delays from lender
  • Access issues / tenant coordination
We’ll identify these early so you’re not surprised.

What speeds it up

  • Clear title and payoff info
  • Quick access for evaluation
  • Clear decision-maker(s)
  • Responsive communication
We’ll help you build a clean closing checklist.

Quick checklist

  • Owner name(s) and best contact
  • Property address + occupancy status
  • Rough mortgage balance (if any)
  • Any liens/judgments you know about
  • Your ideal close date

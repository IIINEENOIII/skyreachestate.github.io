The SkyReach process

  1. Short call (10–15 min): understand your goal and constraints.
  2. Property review: quick walk-through or remote review depending on situation.
  3. Strategy + numbers: cash offer and/or alternative structures if they fit.
  4. Closing plan: title work + payoff coordination + chosen timeline.
  5. Close and move on: you pick the date; we handle the details.
If foreclosure is imminent, speed matters. We can move fast, but we’ll always be honest about what’s realistic.

Solutions built around your constraints

Real situations need real options.

Here are the most common homeowner scenarios we solve — and how the process typically works.

Foreclosure / Notice of Sale

Goal: stop the clock, avoid a forced auction outcome, preserve options.

  • We confirm your sale deadline and payoff/reinstatement numbers.
  • We evaluate: fast cash close, creative structure (when viable), or referral to housing counseling/legal support.
  • If a sale is best, we move quickly with title and lender coordination.
If you have a sale date, contact us immediately — options shrink as the date approaches.

Bankruptcy / Financial Hardship

Goal: work within court and attorney timelines to create the cleanest exit.

  • We can coordinate with your attorney (or encourage you to consult one).
  • We plan around lien/payoff reality so there are no closing surprises.
  • We keep everything documented and time-bound.
We’re not attorneys. If you’re in an active bankruptcy, always follow your counsel’s direction.

Inherited / Probate

Goal: reduce burden on the family and simplify the sale.

  • As-is offers, even with cleanout needed.
  • We coordinate with decision-makers/representatives as needed.
  • We can help align timelines with probate milestones.

Major Repairs / As-Is Sale

Goal: avoid contractors, showings, and long renovation timelines.

  • We evaluate repairs realistically and build an as-is number.
  • No inspection “games” — we explain the math.
  • You choose the close date.

Tenant / Landlord Issues

Goal: reduce stress and move on cleanly.

  • We review lease situation and closing constraints.
  • We can buy with tenants in place depending on terms.
  • We outline realistic next steps and risks.

Vacant / Problem Property

Goal: stop carrying costs (taxes, insurance, utilities, vandalism risk).

  • Remote evaluation options available.
  • Quick close to reduce holding costs.
  • Simple process, minimal disruption.

What you can expect

  • No-pressure call: we listen first, then propose options.
  • Transparent numbers: we explain how we get to the offer.
  • Clean closings: we use reputable title/escrow partners.

Investor-led • Solutions-first

SkyReach is built on one idea: people deserve clear options.

We’re a Texas-based real estate investing group focused on helping homeowners navigate stressful situations with straightforward communication, transparent numbers, and a clean closing plan.

What we do

  • Buy homes as-is for cash when speed and certainty matter.
  • Offer alternative structures when they truly help (and only when appropriate).
  • Support sellers facing foreclosure, bankruptcy, inherited properties, major repairs, vacancy, or landlord issues.
We don’t do pressure tactics. We do clear options, real math, and a plan you can trust.

Who we serve

  • Homeowners who need to sell fast
  • Families handling inherited homes
  • Owners with deferred maintenance or vacancy issues
  • People navigating financial hardship
  • Landlords wanting to exit cleanly
